To craft the music, we wanted a low-bit retro sound, but with a modern feel to it.
So we used FL Studio, which has some useful stock percussions that we slightly modified for the beat,
and we used a modern digital synthesizer (VST) called Poizone for a beefy bassline.
On top of that, we used Medusa 2, a VST that emulates a 4-bit synthesizer
and can make fun arpeggios with the notes you give to it.
